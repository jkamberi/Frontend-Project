Description

iPhone, forged from titanium

iPhone 15 Pro Max is the first iPhone to feature an aerospace-grade titanium design, using the same alloy used by spacecraft for missions to Mars.

Titanium has one of the best strength-to-weight ratios of any metal, making these Pro models the lightest ever. You will notice the difference the moment you get one of these.

The beautiful, fine brushed finish on the titanium bands is achieved by precision machining, grinding, brushing and sandblasting.

New contoured edges and the thinnest bezels ever on iPhone make it even more comfortable to hold in your hand.

The titanium band is connected to a new aluminum inner frame via solid state diffusion. This is an industry first, using a thermo-mechanical process that joins these two metals together with incredible strength.

The inner frame is also made from 100% recycled aluminium, which contributes to the overall use of recycled materials and helps us meet our 2030 climate targets.

A display of true intelligence

iPhone 15 Pro Max features an advanced Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion. It boosts refresh rates to 120Hz when you need great graphics performance. And it slows down to save power when it's not needed.

Always-On display

With the Always-On display, the lock screen remains visible so you don't have to touch it to always be in the know. Using smart algorithms, iPhone detects when it's in your pocket or face down and turns off to save battery.
